Privacy Policy

Application: IconAlbums
Effective Date: 2025-12-08
Last Updated: 2025-12-08

1. Information Collected

IconAlbums does not collect personally identifiable information. The app has no analytics, advertising SDKs, or background telemetry.

2. Local Data

  • Album titles, photo references, and purchase state are stored locally using Room and DataStore.
  • When you crop an icon, the generated PNG is saved inside the app’s private storage.
  • All information remains on the device. Nothing is uploaded to developer-operated servers.

3. Photo Access

  • Photos are chosen via the system Photo Picker (Android 13+) or the Storage Access Framework (Android 12 and below).
  • The app retains only the content URI returned by the picker so it can display the photo later.
  • If the source photo is removed or access is revoked, the thumbnail will no longer load until you reselect the file.

4. Network Communication

  • The app itself makes no direct network requests.
  • Google Play handles all purchase and restoration flows via the Play Billing Library.
  • Policy and documentation buttons in the Settings screen open in an in-app viewer.

5. Permissions

  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 12 and below) is requested only to let the Storage Access Framework deliver photos.
  • No storage permission is needed on Android 13 and above because the Photo Picker grants temporary access automatically.
  • The app does not request microphone, camera, or location permissions.

6. Third-Party Disclosure

No user data is shared with third parties. Purchase details processed by Google Play remain under Google’s privacy policy.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

  • You can clear the app’s data or uninstall it to remove all albums, cached icons, and purchase flags.
  • Pinned launcher shortcuts may remain until you remove them from the launcher.

8. Children’s Privacy

IconAlbums is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

Policy updates will revise the “Last Updated” date and the latest version will be published in this view.
